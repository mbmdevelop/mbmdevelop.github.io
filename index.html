About Me

Hi 👋 I am Mario, a Spanish games programmer.

I am always seeking new ways to improve my skills professionally and personally, due to in this profession you never stop learning. For this reason, I like to be always doing projects alone or with others doing my best in every situation to achieve the best possible result.

I am eager to see all the things I can bring to the world of video games but my real passion is to work side by side with other people to create fresh and exciting mechanics and features for new video games, so who knows, maybe we could end up working together.
