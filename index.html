About Me

I'm a Spanish videogame programmer who has a BSc degree in Computer Science for Games at Sheffield Hallam University and a BTEC Level 5 HND in Computing and System Development at ESAT. During those years I have been improving my work and communication skills, becoming involved in the development of projects such as Old Town Stories(Oculus Rift, PC, Steam) or Bouncing Brudas (PC).

Through all the different academic projects that I have done, I have gained experience in video game programming in several platforms and languages, as well as the different parts of video game development. I am an Artificial Intelligence enthusiast eager to learn all that it can bring to the world of video games but my real passion is to work side by side with other people to create fresh and exciting mechanics and features for new video games.

I'm always seeking new ways to improve my skills professionally and personally, because I think in this profession you never stop learning. For this reason, I like to be always doing projects alone or with others always helping with my enthusiasm and perseverance.

Currently, I'm developing my final year personal project focused on a plug-in in Unreal Engine 4 which allows easy and flexible management of Artificial Neural Networks (both in C++ and Blueprints) allowing the option to choose between several learning algorithms such as Backpropagation, Genetic Algorithm or Deep Q Learning Algorithm.
